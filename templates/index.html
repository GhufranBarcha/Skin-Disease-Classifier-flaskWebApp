What We Offer

Skin Disease Identification

Our advanced machine learning model is capable of accurately identifying a wide range of skin diseases, including but not limited to:

  • Actinic Keratosis
  • Basal Cell Carcinoma and other Malignant Lesions
  • Atopic Dermatitis
  • And many more...

With our system, you can upload an image of the affected skin area, and our model will provide a quick and reliable diagnosis.

How It Works

Using our skin disease identification system is easy and efficient:

  1. Click the "Upload Image" button in the previous section.
  2. Select an image of the affected skin area from your device.
  3. Our model will analyze the image and provide a diagnosis within seconds.
  4. You'll receive a detailed report that includes the detected condition and recommended actions.

Our system uses state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms trained on a vast dataset of skin images to ensure accurate results.
